How a round works

1

Start a round

One tap. Everyone in the group gets a random task and the clock starts ticking.

2

Capture the proof

Front and back camera at once — BeReal-style — or a quick video. No gallery uploads, no faking it.

3

The group votes

Thumbs up, thumbs down, and comments. Your friends are the judges. Good luck.

4

Losers pay up

Miss the deadline and the round's fastest finishers pick your punishment for next round.
